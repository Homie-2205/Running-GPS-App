Work with love

My application is based on the calorie calculation formula: Calories = Body weight (kg) × Distance (km) × Coefficient. This is the simplified formula integrated into the code; it is used by fitness experts and researchers. I hold a bachelor's degree in English from the University of Florida (USA) and a certification in computer science from SENA, among other qualifications.

I am passionate about fitness and running. I use my apps, and they work wonders. Many people around the world inspire me and contribute positively to this project. Seeing my well-defined chest, running, doing CrossFit, functional training, or circuit training—it all makes me feel good. And the support from influencers and YouTubers like Weyni Tesfai (Germany), Lily Petals (South Korea), Ashley (USA), and many others makes me even happier. Words have a comforting power.

Principles and values
