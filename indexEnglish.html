My applications are based on the calorie calculation formula: Calories = Body Weight (kg) × Distance (km) × Coefficient. This is the simplified formula, embedded in the code. GPS Running App


Influenced by the words of American entrepreneurs and the Korean YouTuber Lily Petals, and drawing on my SENA degree in Information Systems, my English, and a healthy lifestyle, I decided to share this innovative project with you, aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals. From a sustainable development perspective, respectful of the environment and focused on innovation, like Singapore, this GPS tracker measures distance traveled and time—essential data for athletes. It is suitable for walking, running, jogging, cycling, and many other activities.
